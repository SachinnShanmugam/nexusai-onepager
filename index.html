Core Capabilities

Everything your team needs to never lose a customer again

📥

Unified Inbox — All 12 Channels

WhatsApp, Email, Phone, Instagram DM, Facebook Messenger, Telegram, SMS, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Google Business, Website Chat & Web Forms — every message in one searchable timeline per customer. Zero app-switching.

🧠

AI Conversation Summaries

Every chat, call transcript, and email thread auto-summarized: what was discussed, what was promised, open questions, and the recommended next action — ready before each touchpoint.

🔔

Context-Aware Follow-Up Reminders

AI reads intent signals like "I'll think about it" or "call me Friday" and auto-schedules the exact right follow-up for the exact right rep — no manual reminder needed, ever.

✅

Automatic Task Creation

When a customer says "send me the proposal" or "book a demo", NexusAI instantly creates a CRM task — assigned, due-dated, and linked to the full conversation thread. No rep types a word.

⚙️

Reply-Based Custom Automations

Build no-code workflows triggered by what customers actually say. Example: reply "interested" → move to Qualified → assign senior rep → send brochure → schedule 48hr follow-up. Your logic, fully automated.

🤖

AI Auto-Replies & Draft Suggestions

Handle pricing queries, FAQs, availability, and order status 24/7 in your brand's voice. Or let AI draft replies for human approval — one click to send across every channel simultaneously.

📊

Lead Scoring & Smart Prioritization

AI scores every inbound lead by urgency, intent keywords, channel source, and response history. Your team sees a live ranked list — always knows who to call first and exactly why.

🔗

CRM / ERP Sync — Zero Manual Logging

Conversations, notes, tasks, and deal stage changes sync automatically to Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, SAP, Tally, or your custom ERP. Reps never type a single log entry again.

📈

Analytics, Sentiment & Reports

Track response times, channel performance, rep productivity, deal velocity, and customer sentiment across every channel in one dashboard. Make data-driven decisions, not gut-feel ones.
